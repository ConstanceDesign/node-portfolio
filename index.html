About Me

I have 18 years of experience in graphic design and am a Registered Graphic Designer ("RGD") in Ontario, Canada. I have always excelled in art since early in grade school. My attention to minute details has always stood out as well. I also was known for painting the Mona Lisa on a fingernail. In my esthetics career previous to design, I also had my nail art featured in a publication called "NailPro Magazine." I have illustrated the first book in a series of professionally published children's book, called "The Greenhouse Kids" and it became a best seller. I have also helped write, produce and direct an online commercial and tutorial series for the ULT-G GPS Golf Watch for TecTecTec ! - a company out of France specializing in optics products primarily for the golf and hunting industries. At this point in my career, I have realized my passion is web development. In 2008, I worked with a company called webplanet.ca as a front-end designer, designing custom user friendly websites, including one for the YQG Airport in Windsor, Ontario. My focus was UI and UX before it was called UI and UX! My designs would then go to the programmer to be input into custom CMS (before there was WordPress). It was a fantastic opportunity. Then for the last decade I worked on WordPress websites. I felt limited in my skillset and above all other aspects of marketing and design - such as social media, print, and illustration, websites and coding is what I enjoy the best. I decided I needed a change and wanted to be able to do as much as I possibly could in web development on my own and understand how it worked behind the scenes. I enrolled in the UofT Coding Bootcamp Program and it has been outstanding! I can merge my design and web development skills. Upon completing this program, I will become a "unicorn" junior full-stack developer. I can't wait! I feel I have made the best choice to take my career to the next level.
